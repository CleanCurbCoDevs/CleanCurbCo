# Clean Curb Co. Legal Document Set

This package contains ready-to-paste Next.js `page.tsx` legal pages for Clean Curb Co., operated as a DBA/brand under Stonebranch Capital LLC.

## Included public legal/policy pages

1. Privacy Policy
2. Terms of Service
3. Cancellation, Rescheduling & Refund Policy
4. Payment Authorization & Billing Policy
5. Service Policy & Service Limitations
6. Cookie & Analytics Policy
7. Text, Phone & Email Communications Policy
8. Accessibility Statement
9. Door-to-Door / Home Solicitation Cancellation Notice
10. Commercial Services Addendum
11. Photo & Media Release
12. Employee & Contractor Field Safety Policy

## Customer-facing priority before launch

Highest priority pages for the public footer and booking flow:

- Privacy Policy

- Payment Authorization & Billing Policy

## Conditional / operational documents

- Door-to-Door / Home Solicitation Cancellation Notice: use before in-person residential sales.
- Commercial Services Addendum: use before HOA, apartment, property manager, business, or multi-bin jobs.
- Photo & Media Release: use before identifiable marketing photos/videos/testimonials.
- Employee & Contractor Field Safety Policy: use before anyone performs route work, equipment work, or customer-site work.
