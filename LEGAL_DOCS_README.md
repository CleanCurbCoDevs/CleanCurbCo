## Suggested booking checkbox language

Use separate required checkboxes where possible:

1. `I confirm my bins will be empty, accessible, and placed at the curb or agreed service location by my scheduled route time.`
2. `I agree to the Terms of Service, Service Policy, Payment Policy, and Cancellation & Refund Policy.`
3. `I authorize Clean Curb Co. / Stonebranch Capital LLC to securely save my payment method and charge it only after my route/service is confirmed, and for future recurring visits if I choose a recurring plan.`
4. `I agree to receive service-related texts, calls, and emails about my booking, route timing, reminders, payment links, service updates, and account support. Message/data rates may apply.`
5. Optional marketing checkbox, not pre-checked: `I would like to receive occasional promotions or neighborhood route updates by text or email. I understand consent is not required to book service and I can opt out at any time.`

## Optional media checkbox language

Use only if you want identifiable photos/videos/testimonials for marketing:

`I give Clean Curb Co. permission to use approved before-and-after photos, videos, reviews, or testimonials from my service for marketing. I understand Clean Curb Co. will try to avoid showing faces, license plates, house numbers, or private details unless I specifically approve that use.`

## Company cancellation language update

The Cancellation & Refund Policy, Terms, and Payment Policy now use the preferred refund-or-reschedule concept:

- If the customer has not been charged yet, they will not be charged for an unperformed company-canceled visit.
- If payment was already collected for that visit, Clean Curb Co. will either issue a full refund for the unperformed visit or communicate with the customer to reschedule service.
- If rescheduling cannot reasonably happen or the customer declines rescheduling, the amount paid for that unperformed visit should be refunded unless a different resolution is required or allowed by law.

## Launch-stage note

The policies intentionally avoid saying “licensed and insured.” Add that only once Clean Curb Co. is actually licensed/insured for the relevant service area and operations.

## Attorney review
